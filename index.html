Biography

I study at the Berlin School of Library and Information Science of the Humboldt-University of Berlin, majoring in the master’s degree Information Science. Furthermore, I work as a student assistant in the department for Information Behavior at the same school. I finished my undergraduate degree with my bachelor thesis ‘Information Seeking Behavior of Refugees - A Video Analysis’. The thesis was a great milestone in my life, with which I discovered my strong interest in Information Behavior research. However, since I also enjoy the topics of Information Retrieval, so much so that I would like to engage in more research concerning that field, in order to further specify my research interests.
